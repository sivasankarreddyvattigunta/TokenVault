Guide to Change Network

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Open MetaMask

Click on the MetaMask extension icon in your browser to open MetaMask.

Step 2: Select Network

Click on the network dropdown at the top of MetaMask. It will display the currently selected network (e.g., Ethereum Mainnet).

Step 3: Add/Change Network

Scroll down to the bottom of the network list and click on "Add Network" to add a new network or "Switch to [Network Name]" to switch to an already added network.

Step 4: Enter Network Details

If you are adding a new network, enter the details provided in the table below. If switching, confirm the switch when prompted.
